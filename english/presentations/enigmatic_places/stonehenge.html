Stonehenge (England)

It consists of a ring of standing stones

with each standing stone around 13 feet (4.0 m) high

seven feet (2.1 m) wide and weighing around 25 tons


Archaeologists believe it was constructed from 3000 BC to 2000 BC



Stonehenge was a megalithic astronomical observatory incorporating

a calendar based on the Sun and the Moon
